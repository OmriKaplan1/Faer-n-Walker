Turn Your Steps into Adventures

Faerûn Walker transforms your real-world activity into a Dungeons & Dragons-inspired journey through the world of Faerûn.

▶ Try the Live Demo

How It Works

  • Step Tracking: Your real-life steps translate into in-game movement.
  • Quests & Achievements: Earn gold by completing daily goals.
  • Interactive Map: Explore Faerûn and discover points of interest.
  • Character Growth: Level up your adventurer and collect gear.

Azure Architecture

Our project leverages Azure cloud services for scalability and real-time gameplay:

Azure SQL Database: Stores player progress, gold, and steps.
Azure Blob Storage: Hosts monster and gear images.
Azure Functions: Handles logic for quests, achievements, and interactions.
Azure Maps: Provides the interactive Faerûn map with POIs.
Azure SignalR: Real-time updates for steps, gold, and level.
